Schnitzel

It is a dish of Austrian origin, which is boneless steaks or chicken covered with rusk and fried. This dish is considered one of the most famous dishes of Viennese and Austrian cuisine, in Austria it is called the dish (Wiener Schnitzel) meaning (Viennese Schnitzel), traditionally served in Vienna with sardine fillet and a slice of lemon, as it is usually made in Vienna with veal, and served with potato salad on the side.
